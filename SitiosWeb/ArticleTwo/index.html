The personales pronouns can be subject pronouns and object pronouns.

Subject pronouns:

  • I -> I am tired
  • He -> He is smart
  • She -> She is pretty
  • It -> It is a sunny day
  • We -> We are tired
  • You -> You are friendly
  • They -> They are at marcelo's house

Object pronouns:

  • Me -> Can you help me?
  • Him -> Can you see him?
  • Her -> Belongs to her
  • It -> Give it a kick
  • Us -> We are tired
  • You -> I see you
  • Them -> He can help them
